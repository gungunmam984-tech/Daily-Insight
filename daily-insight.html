About Us

Reporting built to be re-read.

Daily Insight is an independent newsroom covering business, technology, world affairs, sports, and culture. We publish fewer stories than the average outlet, and check each one harder. No anonymous "sources say" without a named editor standing behind it.

Founded by a small group of reporters who left larger newsrooms over the same complaint — speed had started to outrank accuracy — Daily Insight runs on a simple rule: a story ships when it's right, not when it's first.
